# Credits

This project is original research around a diagnostics-first COTL online bridge and server-ledger prototype.

External COTLMP work by the GeoB99/COTLMP contributors and the firebirdjsb/COTLMP fork was reviewed as prior art for LAN UX, player sync, save-transfer, and co-op lifecycle guard ideas. Useful findings are documented in `docs/EXTERNAL_COTLMP_REVIEW.md`.

Those projects are not copied wholesale into this workspace. Any adopted ideas should be adapted to this project's server-ledger and bridge-owned-body model.
